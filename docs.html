The calculator uses sophisticated financial modeling to analyze your specific situation:

1. Tax Analysis

Uses 2024 federal tax brackets and your filing status to calculate the precise tax impact of retirement withdrawals. Handles progressive taxation, standard/itemized deductions, and state tax estimates.

2. Loan Optimization

Performs month-by-month amortization analysis to calculate exact interest savings, payment reductions, and time savings for each loan scenario.

3. Risk Assessment

Optional market correction modeling based on historical patterns (2000, 2008, 2020) to provide realistic opportunity cost calculations under different market scenarios.

4. Comprehensive Analysis

Combines all factors (taxes, penalties, interest savings, foregone growth, Roth redirections) into a single net impact analysis to guide your decision-making.
